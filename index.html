FAQ
Common questions
Do my customers need to install anything? ▼
No. Customers just message your WhatsApp number as normal. They won't even know they're talking to an AI unless you tell them.
What if the AI gets something wrong? ▼
Onito only answers from the knowledge you provide. If it's not sure, it tells the customer it'll check and notifies you instead. You can review every conversation in your dashboard.
How long does setup take? ▼
Most businesses are live within 20 minutes. We pre-fill as much as we can from your website, then you review and fill in any gaps.
Can I see what the AI is saying to my customers? ▼
Absolutely. Every conversation is visible in your dashboard in real time. You can also add or update your knowledge base at any time to improve the AI's answers.
What happens if I cancel? ▼
You can cancel anytime with no penalty. Your WhatsApp number and all your data remain yours.
Do you handle phone calls too? ▼
Not yet — Onito is WhatsApp-only at launch. Voice call handling is on our roadmap and coming soon.
